Whole Davao River

This trip will take you down the length of the Davao river through all 7 rapids and end in davao bay. This trip takes about 6 hrs of rafting. We will provide a small lunch during the trip. This is a very technical trip with 2 rapids being the most technical. We advise that all rafters be over the age of 12. There is a option of an overnight trip where you will go down the river to rapids 5 and then camp overnight under the stars. You will wake up in the morning have breakfast and finish the river.

Davao River: Rapids 3 to End

This trip will take you down the length of the Davao river from the 3 repids and end in davao bay. This trip takes about 5 hrs of rafting. We will provide a small lunch during the trip. This is a medium technical trip, We advise that all rafters be over the age of 8. There is a option of an overnight trip where you will go down the river to rapids 5 and then camp overnight under the stars. You will wake up in the morning have breakfast and finish the river.

Davao River: Rapids 5 to End

This trip will take you down the length of the Davao river from 5 rapids and end in davao bay. This trip takes about 3 hrs of rafting. This is not a technical trip and is good for the whole family, however all rafters be over the age of 4.

Simuay River

This trip will take you down the length of the sumay river and end in davao bay. This trip takes about 3 hrs of rafting after an hour drive to the drop off spot. This is not a technical trip and is good for the whole family, however all rafters be over the age of 4.
